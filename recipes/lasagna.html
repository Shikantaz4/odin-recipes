Lasagna recipe

"Lasagna, a timeless Italian culinary masterpiece, is a delectable layered pasta dish that transcends the boundaries of comfort food. This gastronomic marvel combines perfectly cooked sheets of wide, flat pasta with rich and savory layers of luscious ingredients, creating a symphony of flavors that dance on the palate."

Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets
  • 500 g ground meat (a mix of beef and pork)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 800 g crushed tomatoes or tomato puree
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon sugar (to reduce tomato acidity)
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 500 g ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 200 g grated Parmesan cheese
  • 400 g shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves (optional, for garnish)

Steps to make Lasagna

  1. Cook the lasagna sheets according to the package instructions. Set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, cook the ground meat over medium heat until browned. Add the chopped onion and garlic, and cook until tender.
  3. Add the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, sugar, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer for about 20-30 minutes to allow the flavors to blend.
  4. In a bowl, mix the ricotta cheese with the egg and Parmesan cheese.
  5. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  6. In a baking dish, layer with a thin spread of the meat mixture, followed by a layer of lasagna sheets, and then a layer of the ricotta cheese mixture. Repeat until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of shredded mozzarella cheese on top.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for about 30-40 minutes or until the lasagna is bubbly and golden on top.
  8. Allow the lasagna to rest for about 10 minutes before cutting.
  9. Garnish with fresh basil leaves if desired and serve hot.
